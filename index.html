Introduction

Welcome to our groundbreaking project: "Data Science Analysis of the 'MakeupAddiction' Subreddit (2021-2023)". This endeavor dives into the vibrant world of beauty on Reddit, aiming to extract and harness data-driven insights that can transform content strategies and enhance user engagement for influencers and brands alike.

Leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP), we assess the emotional resonance of influencer content, analyze sentiments around hot topics, and trace the evolution of beauty language, providing brands with the pulse of consumer sentiment. Exploratory Data Analysis

  • Data Cleaning

    Our analysis of the 'MakeupAddiction' subreddit, streamlined to 10,000 entries each in submissions and comments, reveals a community trend towards brevity. Columns with minimal informational value were removed, allowing us to focus on the succinct nature of user interaction, indicative of the subreddit's engagement patterns.

  • Visualization

    Our analysis of 9,734 'MakeupAddiction' subreddit submissions uncovers a community engaged in varied dialogue, as indicated by the average comments per post. The rarity of crossposts and the wide range of post scores illustrate a diverse spectrum of user interaction. Thumbnail analysis and the infrequency of gilds add depth to our understanding of content reception, setting the stage for an in-depth exploration of social media dynamics in this beauty-focused community.

  • New Variables & External Data

    Our NLP analysis of the 'MakeupAddiction' subreddit is now augmented with new variables to capture author influence ('is_top_100_author'), engagement peaks ('is_peak_hours'), and content depth ('is_long_text'). Together with six dummy variables identifying prevalent beauty topics, these enhancements facilitate a richer sentiment analysis. Additionally, incorporating external data like Reddit Karma provides a robust measure of user credibility and influence within the community.
